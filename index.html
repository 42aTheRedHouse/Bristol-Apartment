In Bristol during the week?

Made for more than just a weekend away

If you need somewhere for a few nights while working in Bristol, The Red House makes a good base. It suits people in town for projects, training, temporary work, site visits, or meetings, while still working well for leisure stays.

There is Wi‑Fi, a proper kitchen with the basics, self check-in via lockbox, and enough space to feel settled rather than squeezed in.

What you can expect

  • 2 double beds and a sofa for a fifth guest
  • Lockbox access with PIN sent before arrival
  • Basic kitchen: oven, hob, microwave, fridge
  • On-street parking in the area. Spaces can be easier to find earlier in the afternoon than later in the day

Location

Set in Totterdown, close to Temple Meads and within easy reach of the city

The Red House sits at 42a Richmond Street in Totterdown. It works well if you want a practical Bristol base with the station nearby and the centre within easy reach on foot.

The outside space is a bonus too: a small garden with grass, a paved area by the doors, and a view that gives the place a bit more breathing room than you might expect.
